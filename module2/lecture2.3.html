Good Data Visualizations are...

  1. Truthful: Evidence based.
  2. Functional: Useful!
  3. Beautiful: Aesthetic & User-centered.
  4. Insightful: Reveals things that are hard to see.
  5. Enlightening: The path to knowledge.


Alberto Cairo, 2016, A Truthful Art.

Activity: Data Storytelling

Explore a dataset.
  • Farm sector financial indicators: 2014 - 2021. Also: Dataset info
  • Monthly sales of food at home & away from home: 1997 to 2021. Also: Dataset info.
  • Average monthly participation in the Women, Infants, and Children (nutrition) program, 1980 to 2020. Also: Dataset info.
Pick a story type.
  • Factoid (Outlier): Why does this data point stand out from the others?
  • Interaction (Correlation): Why do these data change with each other?
  • Comparison: What is the meaningful difference between these parts?
  • Change: What made this part of the data change from this to that?
  • Personal: How does this data connect to the lives of the audience?

Fill out the appropriate story worksheet.
